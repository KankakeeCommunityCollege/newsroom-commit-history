Pack the Place Tuesday, September 18 and show your support for the Lady Cavaliers volleyball team!<br>Admission to the game will be free, and there will be free refreshments while supplies last.<br>The game will be played in the KCC Ryan Gymnasium. The Lady Cavaliers volleyball team will play Malcolm X College at 6:30 p.m.<br>"Wear your "Scarlet Fever" T-shirt, to show your school pride. Don't have shirt? The main information desk on the first floor will sell them during the week before the event for $5. T-shirts also will be sold at the game. For information phone Linh Williams, student life coordinator, at 815-802-8628.<br>Optional casual day<br>To participate in the casual day September 17, employees should wear a Scarlet Fever T-shirt, or a collared shirt with "Kankakee Community College" on it, or a business casual shirt and a KCC name badge or staff ID.

It is a casual day for employees who follow the casual day guidelines. View those [guidelines here](http://www.kcc.edu/FacultyStaff/update/Documents/CasualDenimGuidelines.pdf).
