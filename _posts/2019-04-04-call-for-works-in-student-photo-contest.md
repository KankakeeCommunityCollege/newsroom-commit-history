Entry notes:

* Submissions are limited to three per KCC student artist.
* All work must be original photographs of the artist.
* Photos selected for display may be subject to editing to produce and enlarge image.
* Most photos will be reproduced at a minimum of 20 inches by 30 inches. Any work accepted will be displayed in the Hammes Caf&eacute;â€™ gallery for a minimum of one month, and will include the artist and title.
